Aarna Offers ...

Aarna Homes consists of two homestays — one air-conditioned and the other non-AC. Each has two bedrooms, a kitchen, and parking space. Located in the heart of Mangalore city, Aarna is just 1.5 km from the railway station and 1 km from the bus stand.

We cater exclusively to families. Whether you are on your way to the Mookambika Temple, on a vacation, or a pilgrimage, Aarna Homes offers a comfortable and peaceful interim stay.
